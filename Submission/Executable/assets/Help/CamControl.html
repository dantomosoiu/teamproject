Camera Controls

Keyboard & mouse Controls

Q - Raise Camera
W - Move Forwards
A - Move Left
S - Move Backwards
D - Move Right
Z - Lower Camera
Click and hold in canvas and move mouse - Adjust camera direction.

Control Panel

If enabled in the Advanced settings the camera control panel will be shown below the basic display settings.

The four arrows allow movement forwards, backwards left and right. The two buttons with double headed arrows allow movement upwards and downwards.
Click the buttons once to start moving and again to stop.
